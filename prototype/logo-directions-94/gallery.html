Prototype — logo direction (issue #94)

TFI Live needs a mark it can live with at 16px and at 256px.

Three structurally different concepts for the tfi_live icon — each holds up as a flat glyph small enough for the HA sidebar and distinct enough not to fold into a beige transit-app blur.

Note: this adapts the usual variant-switcher pattern — there's no app route to host the icon in, and logos are judged side by side rather than one at a time, so all three concepts render together below instead of behind a bottom-bar toggle. Use the light/dark control to check each mark against both sidebar themes.

home-assistant/brands requirements

icon.png 256×256 required. Square, transparent bg, no padding baked in.
icon@2x.png 512×512 recommended, same art at 2×.
logo.png ≤512 wide optional wide lockup (icon + wordmark), min height 128px.
logo@2x.png ≤1024 wide optional, 2× of logo.png.
*_dark.png — optional dark-theme variants of any of the above.
location — as of the Brands Proxy API (2026.3+), a local custom_components/tfi_live/brand/ folder satisfies this — no home-assistant/brands PR needed (see #82, #97).
